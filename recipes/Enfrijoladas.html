Enfrijoladas


Enfrioladas is a dish that is very simple to make and the Ingredients are not expensives to get.
Since the dish is very simple there are many things that you can add.

Ingredients

  • corn torillas
  • Beans
  • Queso Fresco
  • choice of oil

Steps to create

  1. The first step is cook the tortilla and make sure not to fry it just cook it in the oil and also cook the beans at the same time
  2. Second step is to smash the beans if not already smashed and get a plate and lay out the torillas on it
  3. Third step is to get a spoon of the beans and rub it on both side of the tortilla
  4. Finally you want to put cheese on the tortilla and roll it, After that push some beans over the rolled tortillas and add some more cheese
