Quesadillas


Quesadillas are a simple dish to create at home with very little Ingredients and knowledge of cooking

Ingredients

  • big flour tortillas
  • shredded parmesan cheese
    • Optional Ingredients
  • your choice of meat
  • your choice of vegetables

Steps to create

  1. The first step is to cook the flour tortillaon both sides
  2. Then after both sides of the tortilla is cook you have to add the cheese and let cheese melt for 1 min
  3. After that you are going to want to fold the tortilla in half and let toast on both sides
  4. Finally if you have extra Ingredients to add you want to the Quesadilla and add in tthe rest of your Ingredients
